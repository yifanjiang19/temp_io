About

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Xun Huang (黄勋 in Chinese, pronounced as /shuun hwang/). I am a second-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Cornell University, affiliated with the newly established Cornell Tech. I am advised by Professor Serge Belongie. My research interests include machine learning (especially deep learning) and its applications to computer vision.

I am a recipient of NVIDIA Fellowship 2018-2019 (11 selected worldwide). Thanks NVIDIA!
